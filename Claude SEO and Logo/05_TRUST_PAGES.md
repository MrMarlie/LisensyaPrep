# About LisensyaPrep

LisensyaPrep started with a simple frustration.

Preparing for the PRC board exam in the Philippines shouldn't feel like hunting for scattered resources across Facebook groups, outdated PDF files, and review center handouts you had to borrow from a friend. But for hundreds of thousands of Filipino exam takers every year, that's exactly what the experience looks like.

We built LisensyaPrep to fix that.

## What We Do

LisensyaPrep is a free online exam prep platform built specifically for Filipino professionals taking PRC board exams. We create structured, subject-specific reviewers, practice tests, study guides, and application guides for:

- **Nursing** — Philippine Nurse Licensure Examination (PNLE / NLE)
- **Criminology** — Criminologist Licensure Examination (CLE)
- **Education** — Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET)
- **Pharmacy** — Pharmacy Licensure Examination (PLE)
- **Medical Technology** — Medical Technologist Licensure Examination (MTLE)
- **Agriculture** — Agriculture Licensure Examination (ALE)

Every piece of content we publish is written or reviewed by a licensed professional in that field — not a generic content writer who has never taken a board exam.

## Our Promise to You

We will never charge you to access our reviewer content. The free tools stay free.

We update our coverage guides and exam schedules after every PRC announcement, so you're always working with current information.

We write honestly. If a subject is hard, we'll tell you it's hard. If a common study habit doesn't actually work, we'll say so.

## Our Team

LisensyaPrep is built and maintained by a small team of licensed Filipino professionals and educators based in the Philippines. Our contributors include registered nurses, criminologists, teachers, pharmacists, and medical technologists who have personally passed the board exams they write about.
